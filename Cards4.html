Seasons

Autumn

Autumn, often known as fall, is a season of change and transition between the bright, lively days of summer and the cold months of winter.

Winter

Winter is one of the four seasons and it is the coldest time of the year. The days are shorter, and the nights are longer. Winter comes after autumn and before spring.

Summer

Summer is the hottest of the climatic seasons. It occurs after spring and before autumn. The days are the longest and the nights are the shortest during the summer season

Rainy

The rainy season, also known as the monsoon, is a period of increased precipitation that can bring many changes to the climate and environment
